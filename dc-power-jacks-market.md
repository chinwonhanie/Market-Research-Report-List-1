<p><strong>The DC Power Jacks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Hole DC Power Jacks</li><li>Surface Mounting DC Power Jacks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DC power jacks market is segmented into two primary types: through-hole and surface mount. Through-hole DC power jacks are designed for insertion into pre-drilled holes on circuit boards, providing robust mechanical support and reliability. In contrast, surface mount DC power jacks are soldered directly onto the board's surface, allowing for a more compact design and efficient space utilization. Each type caters to different application needs, with through-hole favored for durability and surface mount preferred for modern, miniaturized electronic devices.</p></p>
