<p><p>SMD Zener diodes are categorized based on the number of diodes in a package: single, dual, and triple/triple opposing types. Single SMD Zener diodes are designed for applications requiring voltage regulation or voltage reference. Dual SMD Zener diodes provide two Zener diodes in one package, ideal for voltage clamping in both directions. Triple/triple opposing SMD Zener diodes feature three diodes, allowing for versatile voltage clamping and protection in complex circuits, catering to diverse design requirements and space constraints in electronic devices.</p></p>
