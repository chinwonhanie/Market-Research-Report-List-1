<p><p>The plastic machinery market is diverse, encompassing various types of equipment essential for plastic manufacturing. Injection molding machines create complex shapes by injecting molten plastic into molds. Extruder machines produce continuous plastic profiles by forcing material through a die. Blow molding machines form hollow plastic items by inflating molten material within a mold. Rolling mill machines are used for processing polymers into sheets or films. Additionally, the "Others" category includes ancillary equipment such as 3D printers and recycling machinery, catering to specialized needs.</p></p>
