<p><p>A tankless hot water heater, also known as an on-demand water heater, heats water directly without the use of a storage tank. When a hot water tap is turned on, cold water travels through a pipe into the unit, where it is heated by gas or electricity. This technology offers numerous advantages, including energy efficiency, a continuous supply of hot water, and a smaller footprint compared to traditional tank systems. </p><p>The Tankless Hot Water Heater Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increased demand for energy-efficient solutions and rising awareness of water conservation. <p><p>The tankless hot water heater market is primarily divided into two types: gas and electric models. Gas tankless heaters use natural gas or propane to heat water on demand, offering faster heating and higher flow rates, ideal for larger households. In contrast, electric tankless heaters are more compact and easier to install, making them suitable for smaller spaces. Both types provide energy efficiency by eliminating standby heat loss, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers seeking reliable, on-demand hot water solutions.</p></p>
