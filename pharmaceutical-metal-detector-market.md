<p><p>The pharmaceutical metal detector market encompasses various technologies, notably Balanced Coil Systems and Ferrous-in-Foil Systems. Balanced Coil Systems detect metal contaminants by utilizing coils positioned to identify variations in magnetic fields caused by metal particles, ensuring high sensitivity and accuracy. Conversely, Ferrous-in-Foil Systems are designed specifically for detecting ferrous metals within foil packaging, utilizing advanced signal processing to minimize false alarms. Both systems are crucial in ensuring product safety and compliance in pharmaceutical manufacturing and packaging processes.</p></p>
