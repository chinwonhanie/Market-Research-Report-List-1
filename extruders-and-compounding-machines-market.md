<p><p>Extruders and compounding machines are vital in processing materials like plastics and rubber. The market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Single Screw Extruders**: Utilize a single screw for material transport and melting, ideal for simple processes and lower viscosity materials.</p><p>2. **Twin Screw Extruders**: Feature two intermeshing screws, providing better mixing and compounding capabilities, suitable for complex formulations and high viscosity materials.</p><p>3. **Ram Extruders**: Use a reciprocating ram to push material through a heated chamber, typically employed in producing high-viscosity products and specialty applications.</p></p>
