<p><p>The aboveground fuel tanks market is primarily categorized into single wall and double wall tanks. Single wall tanks consist of a single layer of material, offering a basic solution for fuel storage but lacking containment features. In contrast, double wall tanks include an inner and outer wall, providing enhanced protection against leaks and spills. This secondary containment system is crucial for meeting regulatory requirements and ensuring environmental safety, making double wall tanks the preferred choice for many applications.</p></p>
