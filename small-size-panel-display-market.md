<p><strong>The Small Size Panel Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AMOLED</li><li>LCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small size panel display market predominantly features two main types: AMOLED and LCD. AMOLED (Active Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diode) displays offer vibrant colors and deeper blacks due to individual pixel illumination, making them ideal for smartphones and wearable devices. In contrast, LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) panels rely on backlighting and are known for their energy efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Each technology serves different applications, with AMOLED favored for premium devices, while LCD remains a popular choice for budget-friendly options.</p></p>
