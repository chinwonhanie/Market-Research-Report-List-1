<p><p>A Coalescing Plate Separator (CPS) is an advanced separation technology used primarily in industrial applications to remove suspended oil and solids from water. It employs a series of parallel plates to enhance the coalescence of smaller oil droplets, allowing them to merge into larger droplets that can be easily separated from water. This technology is crucial in ensuring compliance with environmental regulations and improving water quality before discharge or reuse.</p><p>The Coalescing Plate Separator Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.5% during the forecast period. Additionally, technological advancements are leading to the development of more efficient and compact separator designs, making CPS more appealing to a broader range of applications.
