<p><p>Built-Up Roofing (BUR) membrane markets include two primary types: plywood decks and concrete decks. Plywood decks are lightweight and cost-effective, providing good thermal insulation and ease of installation. They are commonly used in residential and light commercial structures. In contrast, concrete decks offer superior durability and fire resistance, making them suitable for larger commercial buildings and areas with extreme weather conditions. Both types are integral components of BUR systems, which consist of multiple layers for enhanced waterproofing and protection.</p></p>
